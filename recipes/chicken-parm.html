Chicken Parmesan

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Flour
  • Garlic Powder
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°
  2. Combine the flour, egg, and breading mixtures in 3 separate shallow pans.
  3. Slice the chicken in half lengthwise to create 2-3 thinner slices.
  4. Dredge completely in the flour mixture.
  5. Dip the chicken in the egg mixture.
  6. Coat the chicken generously in the breadcrumbs.
  7. Heat oil over medium-high heat.
  8. Remove chicken.
  9. Transfer the chicken to a large, light colored baking sheet.
  10. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  11. Bake uncovered for 12 minutes. Broil at 425° for 1-2 minutes.
  12. Garnish with fresh parsley
